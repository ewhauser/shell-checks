#### Here's a snippet or screenshot that shows the problem:

```sh
#!/bin/bash
printf -v 'a[1]' "%s" "33"
echo "${a[1]}"

printf -v "b[1]" "%s" "33"
echo "${b[1]}"

printf -v c[1] "%s" "33"
echo "${c[1]}"
```

#### Here's what shellcheck currently says:
```
Line 9	SC2154: c is referenced but not assigned.
```
#### Here's what I wanted or expected to see:

Third hunk should behave like former ones, no warning, as c[1] is assigned by printf command.
