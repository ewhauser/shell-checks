I'm trying to pipe shellcheck output to less while maintaining color on the display.
No luck with `shellcheck -f tty filename | less -r`: all black and white.
Is is possible?
Thanks!

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2015-01-23T19:11:57Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/294#issuecomment-71247495):

There's no built-in way to force colors to non-ttys, but you can trick shellcheck with `script`:

```
script -q -c 'shellcheck filename' - < /dev/null | less -r
```
