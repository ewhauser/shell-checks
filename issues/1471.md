```sh
#!/bin/sh
type -a ls
```

#### Here's what shellcheck currently says:
Nothing

#### Here's what I wanted or expected to see:
"In POSIX sh, type flags are undefined"

There are no flags for the `type` builtin defined under POSIX sh ([see spec](https://pubs.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/9699919799/utilities/type.html)), and the same is true for dash. Bash, however, allows [-aftpP].
