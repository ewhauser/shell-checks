# [\#318 Issue](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/318) `open`: Recommend $TMPDIR over $TMP, $TEMP, and $TEMPDIR

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/28323?v=4" width="50">[peti](https://github.com/peti) opened issue at [2015-02-23T10:51:07Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/318):

According to https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TMPDIR, `$TMPDIR` is the proper variable to use for finding the shell environment's designated directory for temporary files. Many alternatives exist, but these are actually not covered by any standard (as far as I know), so it would be nice to recommend that people use the canonical choice.

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/1042559?v=4" width="50">[tr00per](https://github.com/tr00per) commented at [2015-02-23T11:01:59Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/318#issuecomment-75523299):

While recommending `TMPDIR` over `TEMPDIR` makes sense in this context, `TMP` or `TEMP` might not be related to directories at all in specific cases. Assuming they always refer to a directory would annoy me. Putting aside the fact, that `TMP` or `TEMP` are not very descriptive names in general.
