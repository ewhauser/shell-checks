#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/39281593?v=4" width="50">[zatuz](https://github.com/zatuz) commented at [2019-07-21T01:44:53Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1485#issuecomment-513512048):

### [Installing a pre-compiled binary](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck#installing-a-pre-compiled-binary)
#install tar.xz
* apt install xz-utils
#after
scversion="stable"
wget -qO- "https://storage.googleapis.com/shellcheck/shellcheck-${scversion?}.linux.x86_64.tar.xz" | tar -xJv 
cp "shellcheck-${scversion}/shellcheck" /usr/bin/
shellcheck --version
