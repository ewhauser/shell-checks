#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2016-12-17T03:28:28Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/801#issuecomment-267740059):

Unfortunately there's no general way to statically determine whether a variable will ever be assigned or accessed through indirections like `eval` for non-trivial cases. 

A statement like `eval string1="$1"` may or may not result in assignments to `string1` or `arbitraryOtherVar`, and it may or may not access some variable `var1`, depending on the runtime value of `$1`. ShellCheck doesn't try to guess.
