#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/4673728?v=4" width="50">[audeoudh](https://github.com/audeoudh) commented at [2021-05-17 16:22](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/2179#issuecomment-842459074):

`read -r var; echo "$var"` is the same scenario (`read` assigns `$var`), and is supported (it does not raises SC2154).

While using an uppercase variable, we do not have SC2154 because of `check-unassigned-uppercase` not enabled by default.  With `-o check-unassigned-uppercase`, we do have it.
