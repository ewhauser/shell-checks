#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-09-16T20:59:12Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1328#issuecomment-421832489):

When you download a freshly built binary now, `--version` will still say `0.5.0` and that's what's happened here. This is because I haven't figured out a good way to make `--version` say `0.5.0+3d03b0ab` or `0.5.0+git` or something like that. 

If you download the stable release, `--version` will still say 0.5.0 but it will not include this check.
