#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2015-06-21T19:58:26Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/394#issuecomment-113950214):

The terminal must use UTF-8.

In cmd.exe: `chcp 65001`. It works, but badly:

![image](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/2818758/8273065/199863b6-1812-11e5-83ba-8115cc8292ef.png)

It is better in Powershell ISE, `chcp 65001`  and `[Console]::OutputEncoding = [System.Text.Encoding]::UTF8`:

![image](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/2818758/8273141/a5a49da0-1814-11e5-85ca-cfb5c2913666.png)

It works on Linux because UTF-8 is the default. Without UTF-8, it is the same problem as on Windows:

![image](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/2818758/8273118/fce29348-1813-11e5-9622-2a80babfc94e.png)
