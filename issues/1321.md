#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-08-27T01:22:31Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1321#issuecomment-416089058):

ShellCheck generally does not try to keep up with which flags commands do and don't support, but this is a special case worth a warning since `chmod -r` is interpreted as `chmod u-r` and removes read access.
