#### Here's a snippet or screenshot that shows the problem:

```sh
#!/bin/bash
a=($x)
printf '%s\n' "${a[@]}"
```

#### Here's what shellcheck currently says:

```
Line 2:
a=($x)
   ^-- SC2206: Quote to prevent word splitting, or split robustly with mapfile or read -a.
```

#### Here's what I wanted or expected to see:

```
Line 2:
a=($x)
   ^-- SC2206: Quote to prevent word splitting and pathname expansion, or split robustly with mapfile or read -a.
```
I think, [SC2206](https://shellcheck.net/wiki/SC2206) should mention pathname expansion in the warning message and in the page title in the wiki.
