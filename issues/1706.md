#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/3233724?v=4" width="50">[rillig](https://github.com/rillig) commented at [2019-09-29T05:41:00Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1706#issuecomment-536252179):

According to [POSIX](https://pubs.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/9699919799/utilities/V3_chap02.html#tag_18_13_01), only the `!` character means negation:

> A bracket expression starting with an unquoted \<circumflex> character produces unspecified results.

This could be reason enough to provide another warning. I checked that [the NetBSD /bin/sh supports it](https://github.com/NetBSD/src/blob/467fc66daef1475be08be2436c83615f907ec70f/bin/sh/expand.c#L1926) since [March 2017](https://github.com/NetBSD/src/commit/b56bf878a72d49da2594439a75f5f9b7eebcc51a#diff-c6eaa947a0da30be595a7a04fd1f1808R1523). I'm not sure about the other shells.
