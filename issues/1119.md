#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-02-24T03:22:17Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1119#issuecomment-368195046):

Wow, I'm able to reproduce this on Ubuntu 14.04. This happens even with just `a b c`. The test suite fails dramatically.

I'm extremely reluctant to ever blame the compiler, but I see that this issue appears when compiled with `-O1` but not with `-O0` or in interpreted mode.

It works for me when I add `--enable-optimization=0` to your `cabal install` command.

Another approach is to fetch an officially compiled binary from https://shellcheck.storage.googleapis.com/index.html , which is built with a modern compiler. 7.6.3 is 5 years old.
