#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-10-17T15:35:26Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1364#issuecomment-430677874):

This warning does not happen on the latest commit on shellcheck.net, and is not related to `\$`. Your original code most likely had `\/` in it, which previous versions of shellcheck flagged as being a potential error because `/` does not need escaping in double quotes and leads to the backslash being literal.
