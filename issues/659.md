# [\#659 Issue](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/659) `closed`: Shellcheck does not detect variable usage under let

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/10499375?v=4" width="50">[kromanenko](https://github.com/kromanenko) opened issue at [2016-04-22T21:27:11Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/659):

Test piece of script:
gt_cpus=`cat /proc/cpuinfo |grep processor|wc -l`
let "total_vcpus = gt_cpus \* 3"

Actual result:
$ shellcheck myscript
Line 1:
gt_cpus=`cat /proc/cpuinfo |grep processor|wc -l`
^-- SC2034: gt_cpus appears unused. Verify it or export it.

It would be good if shellcheck could to detect usage of variables under let.
