#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/302055?v=4" width="50">[deryni](https://github.com/deryni) commented at [2015-10-27T01:31:15Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/517#issuecomment-151336505):

This appears to be shellcheck not treating `--` as the end of options and so mis-parsing what `-foo` is doing there. But I'm not sure how safe that assumption on shellcheck's part would be.

That it gets this wrong with `grep -e '-foo' dir/*` is also understandable but perhaps a bit more importantly incorrect (as it is the more portable solution to this problem I believe). Though supporting that requires even more specialized knowledge than understanding `--` requires.
