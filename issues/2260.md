# [\#2260 Issue](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/2260) `closed`: Option --color=auto should check 'dump' & 'unknown' terminals

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/554536?u=3646755b12ce8b3cbbbd5922e1af24c1fd8ed161&v=4" width="50">[return42](https://github.com/return42) opened issue at [2021-06-23 15:16](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/2260):

There are use cases where the conditions of `ColorAuto`:

https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/blob/163b2f12e2076a643e744774c8408370655bfc09/src/ShellCheck/Formatter/Format.hs#L71-L79

is not enough. E.g. when you run shellcheck in a _dump_ or _unknown_ terminal which is the case in emacs and other integrations. 

Colors should not active when the environment variable `$TERM` is `dump` or `unknow`.


#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?u=40d36e49d938fda80abd46d6347b9bace1f73dd4&v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2021-07-25 21:52](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/2260#issuecomment-886262606):

I wasn't able to find anything about "dump" or "unknown", but setting `TERM` to `dumb` or having it unset or empty will now correctly disable colors (in lieu of a full terminfo lookup). Thanks!
