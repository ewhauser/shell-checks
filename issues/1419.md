#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2019-01-06T01:30:37Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1419#issuecomment-451707279):

@kenorb In your case ShellCheck is correct. 

`while -r read param` will just fail with `bash: -r: command not found`, it will never assign `param`.

Use `while read -r param` instead to correctly assign the variable.
