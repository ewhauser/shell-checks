#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/16156011?v=4" width="50">[uplime](https://github.com/uplime) commented at [2018-02-02T18:55:34Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1105#issuecomment-362673320):

Shellcheck isn't about what is valid bash and what isn't. `echo $HOST` is valid bash, but it complains about unquoted variable expansions. `[[` is preferred over `[` in bash for a variety of reasons.
