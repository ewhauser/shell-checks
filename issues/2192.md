#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/25598447?u=73c3f3a041be0ccabd72bb816d5782b30aefe822&v=4" width="50">[TinCanTech](https://github.com/TinCanTech) commented at [2021-03-29 21:46](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/2192#issuecomment-809735707):

What `shellcheck` means is that `>` (and `\>`) are undefined in POSIX,
See: https://pubs.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/9699919799/utilities/test.html#tag_20_128

Sure, `dash` lets you use this but it's not POSIX.

If it bothers you then you can add a `shellcheck` directive to ignore it.

You can also create the wiki page yourself.
