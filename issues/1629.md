# [\#1629 Issue](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1629) `closed`: SC2140 incorrectly triggered with `=` in quoted fixed strings

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/5332158?v=4" width="50">[evandrocoan](https://github.com/evandrocoan) opened issue at [2019-06-30T04:46:53Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1629):


```
eval "${newvariablename}"="'${fixcommand}'";
                         ^-- SC2140: Word is of the form "A"B"C" (B indicated). Did you mean "ABC" or "A\"B\"C"?
```

1. https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1565

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2019-07-01T00:09:11Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1629#issuecomment-507078767):

The suggestion is correct. The inner quotes don't actually do anything. You can just remove them:

    eval "${newvariablename}='${fixcommand}'";

If you prefer your version stylistically, that's a separate matter.
