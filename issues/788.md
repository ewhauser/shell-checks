#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2016-12-10T19:20:13Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/788#issuecomment-266231138):

In 0.3.7, before `dash` support, `ash` was aliased to `sh`. Later this broke, so it defaulted to `bash`.

From fee6c94, `ash` will be aliased to `dash`, and there's a warning saying when this is happening.
