I have written an integration of Shellcheck with Test Anything Protocol.

https://github.com/wbazant/shellcheck-tap-integration

Does it warrant mentioning on the Integration page? It's completely not groundbreaking: it takes the GCC style messages, numbers files passed on input, runs shellcheck, and treats "warnings" and "errors" as failures.

It's just a notch above nontrivial but it will help anyone who had the same problem I had (I needed the functionality for my CI build). You might or might not want it on the wiki page: you mention JUnit only, which is more popular and less trivially integrated.

Quality of the integration - I checked the script with Shellcheck ;) but if someone gave it a review I would be most grateful.
