#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2014-12-25T00:51:27Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/270#issuecomment-68080405):

There's a simple Haskell library wrapper in the module `ShellCheck.Simple`:

```
shellCheck (AnalysisOptions Nothing []) "echo $(date)"
```

However, have you instead considered using the CheckStyle or GCC style output (`-f checkstyle`, `-f gcc`)? Most IDEs will support one or both already.
