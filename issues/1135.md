#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-03-05T00:12:52Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1135#issuecomment-370277838):

Wow. This behavior comes from a very early bugfix where parsing would incorrectly fail for here documents containing `$"`, but there was also a unit test verifying this behavior for backticks for some reason.

It might have been because it's not a syntax error in bash (it's just a bad substitution), and Bash 2 even implicitly terminated them. Failing fast is clearly the better behavior though, and now that's what happens.

Thanks for the bug report, and I'm glad you find ShellCheck useful!
