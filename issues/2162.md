#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/25393735?u=80bca3459bd85ec194fa574a7e0d17692be7ab9b&v=4" width="50">[silverlyra](https://github.com/silverlyra) commented at [2021-03-11 23:41](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/2162#issuecomment-797124207):

I just hit this on a brand-new M1 Mac, and did some digging. The Homebrew formula for shellcheck [specifically depends on `ghc@8.8`](https://github.com/Homebrew/homebrew-core/blob/HEAD/Formula/shellcheck.rb#L18), and [a discussion in homebrew-core](https://github.com/Homebrew/homebrew-core/pull/57892#issuecomment-735851664) ended with “ghc 8.8 may be a lost cause for Apple Silicon.”

If Shellcheck can be compiled under ghc 8.10 or 9, that seems like the path to getting it bottled again for ARM macOS. (There’s still no `ghc@8.10` ARM64 bottle, either, though.)
