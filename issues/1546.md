#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2019-05-13T02:33:23Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1546#issuecomment-491656302):

This issue resolved itself with 5fb1da68, which replaced verbose checks with optional checks.

Instead of getting an indiscriminate pile of suggestions with a new priority, you can instead enable sets of them by name using `-o` or `enable` directives. They reuse existing priority levels.
