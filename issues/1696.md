#### Here's a snippet or screenshot that shows the problem:

```sh
#!/bin/bash
declare -a arr=(1)
[[ arr[0] -gt 0 ]] && echo "Greater than 0"
```

#### Here's what shellcheck currently says:
Line 3:
[[ arr[0] -gt 0 ]] && echo "Greater than 0"
   ^-- SC2203: Globs are ignored in [[ ]] except right of =/!=. Use a loop.


#### Here's what I wanted or expected to see:
No warnings, as this is perfectly valid bash.

This may be related to issue 1641, as that deals with * being interpreted as globbing instead of multiplication causing SC2203 being raised.
