#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-01-16T00:08:35Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1053#issuecomment-357816927):

I was able to reproduce this. It's apparently due to an incompatibility in glibc, which is used for `getpwent` and such even when statically linked. Unfortunately, I don't have any good solutions or workarounds.

I do have a bad workaround though:

    touch /tmp/libc.so.6
    LD_LIBRARY_PATH=/tmp shellcheck

This creates a dummy libc for the binary to try and fail to use, before falling back on its default.

If you base your image off of ubuntu:15.04 or later, this does not appear to be a problem.
