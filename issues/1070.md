#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2017-12-07T19:06:01Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1070#issuecomment-350063705):

The expansion should be quoted, but it should also be an array. This is one of the cases mentioned on the [wiki](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/wiki/SC2086) that the warning itself is too short to describe.

If the goal is to split a string into words, use `read` instead of `set`+wordsplitting.
