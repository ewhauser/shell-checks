#### Here's a snippet or screenshot that shows the problem:

I have a zsh-theme called [Spaceship](https://github.com/denysdovhan/spaceship-zsh-theme) and [we use `EXTENDED_GLOB` option for zsh](https://github.com/denysdovhan/spaceship-zsh-theme/blob/master/spaceship.zsh#L470-L474). Unfortunately, shellcheck fails when it meet [such expressions](https://github.com/denysdovhan/spaceship-zsh-theme/blob/master/spaceship.zsh#L253-L254):

```sh
# Show Pyenv status only for Python-specific folders
[[ -f requirements.txt || -n *.py(#qN) ]] || return
```

```sh

```

#### Here's what I wanted or expected to see:

Parse EXTENDED_GLOB globs as well as others.
