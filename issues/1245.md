#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-06-01T23:35:40Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1245#issuecomment-394035612):

Sorry to break your build! tl;dr: switch to:

    name: koalaman/shellcheck-alpine:stable

GitLab requires its images to have a shell. `koalaman/shellcheck` used to be based on Alpine Linux and therefore just happened to contain one. 

Now it only contains a statically compiled `shellcheck` binary, making it significantly smaller. The `shellcheck-alpine` image is there for anyone who wants a more complete image with extra utilities.
