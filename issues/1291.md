#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-07-28T19:03:08Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1291#issuecomment-408628607):

Sorry, silencing fatal parser errors is purely cosmetic; any parser error found will still stop ShellCheck at the point of the error. There's no great way of resuming parsing of unrecognized code because you don't know what kind of state you'd be in.
