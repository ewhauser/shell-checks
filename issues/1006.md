#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2017-09-15T17:49:16Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1006#issuecomment-329852563):

`dash` does not support `echo -e`:

```
$ dash -c 'echo -e "hello\nworld"'
-e hello
world
```

ShellCheck doesn't support `ash` at this time, so you can either ignore the warning or rewrite it in a `sh` or `dash` compatible way (with `printf`).
