#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?u=40d36e49d938fda80abd46d6347b9bace1f73dd4&v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2021-09-01 18:38](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/2313#issuecomment-910558968):

You're right, see [the wiki page](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/wiki/SC2209). 

Unfortunately it's hard to automatically differentiate it from its equivalent true positive:

```
vars=set
echo "$vars"
```

This is one of several warnings that are intended to help absolute beginners, in this case those who aren't familiar with command substitution. Since you're clearly not in that category it's unlikely to ever be helpful. Please don't hesitate to banish this suggestion in your .shellcheckrc.
