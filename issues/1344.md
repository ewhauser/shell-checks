#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-12-02T21:07:12Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1344#issuecomment-443542106):

Having a space after a redirection operator is valid and causes no problems, so it's a subjective stylistic choice that ShellCheck doesn't aim to get involved in. I would argue that having a space improves readability, and also helps with e.g. `< <(cmd)` where a space is in fact required.
