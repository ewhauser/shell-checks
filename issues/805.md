#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2016-12-20T21:46:32Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/805#issuecomment-268368615):

`bash filename` will always ignore the shebang entirely. There's no way around that.
