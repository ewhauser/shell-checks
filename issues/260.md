# [\#260 Issue](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/260) `closed`: False positive with SC2069 (redirecting stderr to a pipe)

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/71486?v=4" width="50">[asmeurer](https://github.com/asmeurer) opened issue at [2014-11-19T20:30:01Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/260):

`cmd 2>&1 >/dev/null` gives `The order of the 2>&1 and the redirect matters. The 2>&1 has to be last. [SC2069]`. However, this is intentional, as it's the way to pipe stderr (see http://stackoverflow.com/a/2342841/161801).
