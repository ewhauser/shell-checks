#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2017-05-22T00:37:20Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/889#issuecomment-302974446):

ShellCheck tries (and sometimes fails) to be clever about this. 

Since `BASE_PATH` is a constant without metacharacters, quoting doesn't actually matter, so no 2086 is reported.

There should really be either a verbosity option or a separate style/info suggestion though.
