- [ ] I searched through https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues and didn't find anything related


#### Here's a snippet or screenshot that shows the problem:

```sh
#!/usr/bin/env bash

declare -n ref=_

for ref in foo bar baz
do ref=hello
done
```

#### Here's what shellcheck currently says:
```
Line 6	SC2034: ref appears unused. Verify use (or export if used externally).
```
#### Here's what I wanted or expected to see:

`SC2034: ref appears unused` should not be triggered because:
- The nameref variable **is effectively used** to assign a value to its successive referees (`foo`, `bar` and `baz` variable names).
