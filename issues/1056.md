#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2017-11-20T04:00:46Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1056#issuecomment-345585855):

You've already escaped some of the glob characters, but not all of them. You can either escape the rest, or remove the escaping you already have and quote the whole string:

    grep -o viewlongc.php\?id=\[0-9]\*\&chapter=\[0-9]\* < "test.html"
    # or
    grep -o 'viewlongc.php?id=[0-9]*&chapter=[0-9]*' < "test.html"

Adding quotes around the whole thing leads to some double-escaped characters which is why that fails.
