# [\#1806 Issue](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1806) `closed`: SC2034 false positive in heredoc

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/13752208?u=5e2b2f6898135b73828aa4e300377f7ee289fdf6&v=4" width="50">[mvanorder](https://github.com/mvanorder) opened issue at [2020-01-24 22:59](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1806):

shellcheck version: 0.7.0

When a variable is only used in heredoc shellcheck doesn't see it as being used.

#### Here's a snippet or screenshot that shows the problem:

```sh
pub_key=$(cat "${HOME}/.ssh/id_rsa.pub")

ssh jumpserver << 'ENDSSH'
        [ ! -d .ssh ] && mkdir -m700 .ssh
        grep "${pub_key}" .ssh/authorized_keys > /dev/null || echo "${pub_key}" >> .ssh/authorized_keys
ENDSSH
```

#### Here's what shellcheck currently says:

In test line 42:
    pub_key=$(cat "${HOME}/.ssh/id_rsa.pub")
    ^-----^ SC2034: pub_key appears unused. Verify use (or export if used externally).

#### Here's what I wanted or expected to see:

Nothing


#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/215702?u=b9f8c7106217535ccc93e6b7736992b2ef690350&v=4" width="50">[gromgit](https://github.com/gromgit) commented at [2020-01-24 23:23](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1806#issuecomment-578340773):

You quoted the heredoc delimiter. From the `bash` man page:
> If any part of [the heredoc] `word` is quoted, the `delimiter` is the result of quote removal on `word`, and **the lines in the here-document are not expanded**.

Try replacing `ssh jumpserver` with `cat`. You'll see that `$pub_key` is not substituted in the heredoc, and since you don't deference `pub_key` anywhere else in your snippet, SC2034 is perfectly correct.
