#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2014764?v=4" width="50">[kpankonen](https://github.com/kpankonen) commented at [2019-10-31T22:00:46Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1741#issuecomment-548586442):

The `koalaman/shellcheck:stable` image uses shellcheck as the entrypoint.  I think you will need to override the entrypoint as documented here: https://docs.gitlab.com/ee/ci/docker/using_docker_images.html#overriding-the-entrypoint-of-an-image




#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2019-11-03T19:20:09Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1741#issuecomment-549169400):

The `koalaman/shellcheck` image only contains ShellCheck. If you need additional tools like `sh`, `find` or `xargs`, try the `koalaman/shellcheck-alpine` image instead.


#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/217935?v=4" width="50">[AloisMahdal](https://github.com/AloisMahdal) commented at [2019-11-15T16:05:42Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1741#issuecomment-554420743):

Sorry for lack of response.  I've switched to `koalaman/shellcheck-alpine` in my `.gitlab-ci.yml`:

    shellcheck:
        image: koalaman/shellcheck-alpine

This is literally my whole `.gitlab-ci.yml` and it works like a charm!  Thanks!
