# [\#973 Issue](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/973) `open`: sort -V is not portable

#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/464648?v=4" width="50">[austin987](https://github.com/austin987) opened issue at [2017-08-16T19:05:08Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/973):

For a project I needed a portable way to sort version numbers. I had thought I'd  found that in sort -V, but as someone recently pointed out, it's not portable (not on OSX and I assume the BSDs, unsure about Solaris):

Sample code:
#!/bin/sh
printf "%s\\n%s\\n%s" '1.2.3' '7.5.7' '4.5.1' | sort -V

Should be simple to detect and warn about
