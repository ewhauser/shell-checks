#### Here's a snippet or screenshot that shows the problem:

```sh

#!/bin/sh
printf '%s\n' 1 2 | xargs -i echo {}
```

#### Here's what shellcheck currently says:

N/A

#### Here's what I wanted or expected to see:

Some xargs don't have option `-i`. Use `-I{}` instead.

#### Context

- BSD implementations do not support `-i`. Reference: https://www.freebsd.org/cgi/man.cgi?query=xargs
- GNU implementation deprecates `-i`. Reference: https://git.savannah.gnu.org/cgit/findutils.git/commit/?id=0b1f485a14102dfa58ec4eb66260f70e26496430
