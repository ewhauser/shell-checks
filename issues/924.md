#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2017-06-02T23:47:30Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/924#issuecomment-305933226):

This is intended behavior: shellcheck doesn't know that you actually wanted `VAR` to be interpreted as a glob. It would be buggy for `VAR="my file.txt"` or `VAR="The.Matrix.[1999].mkv"`. 

You can either keep disabling SC2086, or you can rewrite it to not construct globs at runtime.
