#### <img src="https://avatars.githubusercontent.com/u/2818758?v=4" width="50">[koalaman](https://github.com/koalaman) commented at [2018-12-17T19:27:52Z](https://github.com/koalaman/shellcheck/issues/1418#issuecomment-447968119):

I see what you mean, though `mktemp` *can* produce filenames with globs/spaces:

    mktemp "/tmp/My File.XXXXXX"
    TMPDIR="/mnt/sdcard/Temporary Files/" mktemp 

It can also output nothing in the event of an error, which is also better off when quoted.

Rather than adding the complexity and introducing a few (rare) failure cases, it might be better to just quote it.
